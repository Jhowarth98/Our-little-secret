How to play
1
Make your cards
Head to the Make screen and write your dare. Pick an icon, category, and optionally set a timer — great for timed dares like a massage. Then add the card to your deck.
2
Shuffle if you like
Tap Shuffle on the home screen to randomise the order of your deck before playing.
3
Spin the reel
Go to Play and tap Draw a Card. The reel spins and lands on a random card from your deck.
4
Complete the dare
Whatever the card says — you have to do it! Tap Draw Again when you're ready for the next one.
Your cards save automatically ♥
